Design: A 4th generation PAMAM Dendrimer based nanocapsule to increase biocompatibility. It contains mutated KRAS siRNA or a CRISPR/Cas9 product. Alternatively, it may contain a normal p53 gene in order to promote apoptosis of cancer cells. The nanocapsule is coated with various targeting ligands which can target receptors much more prominent on pancreatic cancer cells, such as the folic acid receptor.
