“ISTF Project # 15-3056 was developed in response to the 2015-2016 Internet Science and Technology Fair”

Project Title: NanoWarriors

National Critical Technology (NCT): Living Systems

Sub Category: Biocompatible Materials

Specific Technology Application: Microcapsules

50-word Statement:

Pancreatic cancer has almost a 100% mortality rate. Most treatments are ineffective and cannot target the specific area that significantly treats the cancer. Nanocapsules can be created to detect pancreatic cancer and apply treatments that are targeted specifically to the tumor. These will increase the effectiveness of preexisting treatments.


School: Bergen County Academies, New Jersey
